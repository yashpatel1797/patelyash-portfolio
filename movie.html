Movies teach us

1. Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump was released in 1994. There are countless life lessons from Forrest Gump. there are some lessons like Don't be afraid, to be honest, treat everyone exactly the same with equal respect, Always try new things and become great at this, Never takes true friendship for granted, Appreciate and share the little things, Do what you love.

2. The pursuit of happiness

The pursuit of happiness is based on the life of Chris Gardner who lost everything in life and gets everything back on life. He is a hard-working, caring, and loving father. What we can learn from this movie is raising a child is a very difficult task especially when you are financially not stable, Second thing to learn from movie is how he takes care of his son despite having difficult times, he tries to do everything but not able to get success and ended up in a bad situation, people discourage you because they have not succeeded at something.

3. The Shawshank Redemption

In Shawshank redemption there are many lessons which will help us in life, the main thing we learn from this movie is hope is a good thing, maybe the best of all things, plan well ahead and be smart, Persistence pays off- relentlessly chipped of away wall until he had tunneled.
